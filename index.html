A Personal Note

I was raised by my grandparents. They showed me - through quiet, daily sacrifice - what it truly means to love someone. That upbringing is the reason I do this work.

After more than a decade of public service and a career shaped by service in the U.S. Navy, I know what it means to show up for others. I founded Our Futures Past, a nonprofit that brings generations together through free events at senior centers right here in Anne Arundel County - because I believe connection is one of the most powerful things we can give.

I built Comfort Keepers Annapolis on those same values: a professional, well-trained team that treats every client like family. This isn't just a business to me. It's a calling.

Jordan Derby
