Our Services

Compassionate, non-medical companion and homemaker services designed to help seniors and adults maintain independence, safety, and quality of life at home.

✓ Available Now

CC

Companion Services

Meaningful social engagement, supervision, and support for daily routines.

  • Conversation, card games, reading aloud, and hobby engagement
  • Supervision & safety monitoring for cognitive changes or fall risk
  • Appointment escort to medical visits, social outings, and community events
  • Scheduled phone or video check-ins to ensure wellbeing
HM

Homemaker Services

A clean, comfortable, and functional home environment - maintained for you.

  • Vacuuming, dusting, mopping, bathroom/kitchen cleaning, and laundry
  • Nutritious meal planning and preparation based on dietary preferences
  • Grocery runs, pharmacy pickups, post office visits, and household errands
  • Mail sorting, bill organization, and document filing
TR

Transportation & Errands

Reliable, safe transportation for clients who no longer drive or prefer not to.

  • Door-to-door transport within Anne Arundel County for appointments, shopping, and social visits
  • Extended transport beyond county lines (Baltimore, DC metro) with advance scheduling
  • Pharmacy pickups, post office runs, dry cleaning, and other household errands
SS

Safety, Technology & Home Organization

Powered by Our Futures Past, keeping seniors safe at home and in the digital world.

  • Room-by-room home safety walkthrough with written recommendations
  • Decluttering, seasonal organization, pantry/closet systems, and labeling
  • Device setup, troubleshooting, and software updates for smartphones, tablets, and smart TVs
  • Scam prevention, phishing education, AI voice cloning awareness, and password coaching

A Personal Note

I was raised by my grandparents. They showed me - through quiet, daily sacrifice - what it truly means to love someone. That upbringing is the reason I do this work. After more than a decade of public service and a career shaped by service in the U.S. Navy, I know what it means to show up for others. I founded Our Futures Past, a nonprofit that brings generations together through free events at senior centers right here in Anne Arundel County - because I believe connection is one of the most powerful things we can give. I built Comfort Keepers Annapolis on those same values: a professional, well-trained team that treats every client like family. This isn't just a business to me. It's a calling.
